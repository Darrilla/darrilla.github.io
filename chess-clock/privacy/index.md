# Privacy Policy for Chess Clock

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Chess Clock is a mobile chess clock app designed for over-the-board play.

## Information We Collect

Chess Clock does not collect personal information from users.

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide your
name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or payment
information.

## How the App Works

Chess Clock runs locally on your device. Game timing, settings, and app
behavior are handled on-device.

## Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Chess Clock does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking
technologies.

## Data Sharing

Chess Clock does not sell, share, or transfer personal information to third
parties.

## Children's Privacy

Chess Clock does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If Chess Clock's features change in the future, this Privacy Policy may be
updated. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised
"Last updated" date.
